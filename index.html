Privacy Policy

ytNotes Browser Extension

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and protected when using the ytNotes browser extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your data.

Information We Collect

  • Screenshots from YouTube videos that you capture using our extension
  • Notes and annotations you create while watching videos
  • Basic usage analytics to improve our service (no personal data)

How We Use the Information

  • All your data is securely stored in your personal Google Drive under a dedicated ytNotes folder
  • We use this data solely to provide the core functionality of the ytNotes extension
  • Your data remains under your complete control and ownership
  • We do not access, read, or process your personal notes or screenshots

Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Your data is stored securely in your Google Drive account
  • We implement strict access controls and security measures

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties
  • Your data is never used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • We do not share your information with external services except Google Drive (which you control)

Data Deletion

  • You can delete any note or screenshot at any time from within your Google Drive
  • You can also manage your data through the ytNotes dashboard
  • Deleting the extension will not automatically delete your data from Google Drive
  • You have complete control over your data retention
